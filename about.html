About the Author

Hi, I'm Eshita Shukla

A Computer Science graduate exploring the intersection of technology, philosophy and human development.

Beyond technology, I deeply enjoy reading self-help, psychology and philosophical literature that explores discipline, growth and meaning in modern life.

I am also a lawn tennis player, and sports have shaped my understanding of consistency, resilience and mental clarity.

Through this digital space, I aim to create thoughtful conversations around development, human potential and the future of technology in society.
